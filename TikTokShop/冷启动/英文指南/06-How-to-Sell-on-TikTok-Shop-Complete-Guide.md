# How to Sell on TikTok Shop: Complete Step-by-Step Guide (2026)

> Apr 07, 2026

TikTok Shop has grown into a very strong e-commerce platform for sellers. It combines a platform with over a billion users and a built-in checkout that keeps shoppers inside the app. Whether you run a brand, already sell online, and want a new way to earn, or are a creator wanting to make money from your audience, this guide covers every step. It explains eligibility, setting up your account, listing products, making content that sells, and growing with TikTok's affiliate program.

## What Is TikTok Shop and How Does It Work?

TikTok Shop is TikTok's built-in shopping platform. It lets sellers list products and complete sales entirely inside the app, without redirects or lost sales from extra steps. Buyers discover products through content they're already watching, then purchase without ever leaving the app.

There are three core selling surfaces:

| Sales Surface | How It Works | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| Shoppable Short Videos | Product links are tagged directly in a video; viewers tap to purchase | Demos, reviews, unboxings |
| TikTok LIVE Shopping | Products are showcased and pinned during a live stream for real-time purchase | Flash deals, Q&A selling, launches |
| Shop Tab | A dedicated storefront page within TikTok where users browse your full catalog | Brand discovery, repeat buyers |

The checkout, payment processing, and order notification all happen inside TikTok. Before any of that is possible, though, you need to confirm your eligibility and get your seller account approved.

## TikTok Shop Eligibility and Requirements

Before you register, verify that you meet TikTok Shop's baseline requirements. Missing a single document is the most common reason applications are delayed or rejected.

**Identity and Business Documentation (US sellers)**

- Government-issued photo ID (passport or driver's license for individual sellers)
- Employer Identification Number (EIN) for business accounts
- Valid US business license (for registered business entities)
- US-based bank account for payouts
- A TikTok account in good standing (no recent violations or bans)

**Business Type**

- **Individual seller**: Permitted; requires personal ID verification
- **Business entity (LLC, corporation, sole proprietor)**: Requires EIN, business license, and proof of business address

**Product Category Restrictions**

TikTok Shop prohibits or restricts several categories regardless of seller status. Restricted and prohibited categories include firearms and weapons, alcohol and tobacco, live animals, pharmaceutical drugs, regulated health supplements, and counterfeit or intellectual-property-infringing goods. Some categories (e.g., beauty, health, food supplements) require additional documentation or category approval before products can be listed.

**Common Rejection Reasons**

- Mismatched name between ID and bank account
- Blurry or incomplete document uploads
- Business license that covers a different business category than the products you intend to sell
- Operating from a country where TikTok Shop is not yet available

## How to Create a TikTok Shop Seller Account?

Registration happens through TikTok's Seller Center — a dedicated dashboard separate from the main TikTok app. Here is the complete walkthrough.

1. **Go to the Seller Center for your region.**
   - US: seller-us.tiktok.com
   - UK: seller-uk.tiktok.com
   - Southeast Asia: seller.tiktok.com
   
   Navigate to the correct regional portal; applying through the wrong portal will result in a rejection regardless of your documentation.

2. **Log in or create your TikTok account.** You can register using an existing TikTok account, a phone number, or an email address. If you have a business TikTok account already, linking it here keeps your shop connected to your existing content and audience.

3. **Select your seller account type.** Choose either **Individual** or **Business/Corporate**. Individual accounts require a personal government-issued ID. Business accounts require an EIN, business license, and supporting business documentation. You cannot switch between types after approval, so choose carefully.

4. **Enter your business information.** Fill in your business name, address, and contact details exactly as they appear on your legal documents. Discrepancies between your application and uploaded documents are a leading cause of rejection.

5. **Upload verification documents.** Navigate to the **Business Verification** tab and upload:
   - Front and back of your government-issued ID (individual) or business license (business entity)
   - EIN confirmation letter (US business sellers)
   - Bank account details for payouts (account number and routing number)
   
   Ensure all uploads are clear, fully visible, and unexpired.

6. **Set up your shop profile.** Add your shop name, logo, and a brief shop description. This is your storefront identity within TikTok, so use your brand name consistently.

7. **Agree to the seller's terms and submit.** Review TikTok Shop's Seller Agreement, confirm your information is accurate, and submit the application.

8. **Wait for approval.** TikTok typically reviews applications within **1–3 business days**. You will receive an email notification when approved. If your application is rejected, the rejection email will specify the reason — usually a document issue — and you can resubmit through the same portal after correcting the problem.

## How to List Your Products on TikTok Shop?

Once your seller account is approved, listing products is your immediate next step. You have two paths: manual upload or integration sync.

**Manual Product Upload (Step-by-Step)**

1. Log in to Seller Center and navigate to **Products** → **Add New Product**.
2. Select the correct product category from TikTok's category tree. This determines which attributes you'll need to fill in and affects your commission fee rate.
3. Write your **product title** — aim for 60–80 characters, front-load the most important keyword, and include key attributes (brand, material, size, use case).
4. Upload **product images**. TikTok requires a minimum of 1 image; best practice is 6–9 images. The first image appears in the Shop tab and video product cards — make it count.
5. Write your **product description**. Lead with the primary benefit, then cover features, dimensions, materials, and compatibility. Use short paragraphs and bullet points for readability.
6. Enter **pricing**, including any sale/compare-at price if you're running a promotion.
7. Set up **SKUs and inventory** — input variants (size, color, style), stock quantity, and per-variant pricing if applicable.
8. Enter **shipping weight and dimensions** — TikTok uses this to calculate shipping costs and validate carrier compatibility.
9. Save and submit the listing for review. Most listings are approved within a few hours.

**Integration Sync: Shopify and WooCommerce**

TikTok offers a native integration with Shopify through the TikTok channel app in the Shopify App Store. Once connected, your product catalog syncs automatically, and orders placed on TikTok Shop flow back into Shopify's order management system. WooCommerce sellers can use TikTok's API or third-party connector tools for similar functionality. Catalog sync is highly recommended if you sell on multiple platforms.

### TikTok Shop Product Listing Best Practices

Strong listings convert browsers into buyers. Apply these optimization practices from day one:

- **Lead your title with the highest-intent keyword.** Buyers search within TikTok Shop — titles that match search terms rank better in the Shop tab. Example: "Wireless Earbuds Noise Cancelling Bluetooth 5.3" outperforms "Our Premium Sound Product."
- **Make the first image demonstrate the product in use.** Lifestyle/in-context images consistently outperform flat product-only shots on TikTok's commerce surfaces.
- **Write benefit-first descriptions.** Start by explaining what the product does or what problem it solves, then share its features and details.
- **Set competitive pricing from launch.** TikTok's algorithm surfaces products with strong click-through and conversion signals. Pricing too high at launch limits early data.
- **Use TikTok's built-in deals and coupons feature.** Discount badges in the Shop tab increase tap-through rate. Even a modest 5–10% welcome coupon can meaningfully boost early conversion rates.

## How to Create TikTok Content That Drives Sales?

Content drives TikTok Shop. Unlike regular online stores that rely on ads or SEO, sales here mostly come from videos and live streams. Showing your product in context encourages impulse buys, making content the most important factor for sellers.

**Step-by-Step: Creating a Shoppable Product Video**

1. Record or upload your video in TikTok's native camera or the TikTok LIVE Studio. Keep it between **30 and 60 seconds** for most product categories.
2. On the edit screen, tap **Add Link → Products** to tag one or more products from your TikTok Shop catalog. A shopping bag icon will appear on the video.
3. Write a caption that reinforces the video's hook and includes a soft call to action ("Link in video" or "Tap the bag to grab yours").
4. Select relevant hashtags — mix product-specific (#WirelessEarbuds), niche (#TechTok), and discovery-oriented (#TikTokShop) tags.
5. Post and monitor performance in Seller Center analytics under the video-attributed sales metric.

**Content Formats That Convert on TikTok Shop**

- **Problem/solution hook videos**: Open with the problem your viewer has, then demonstrate your product as the fix
- **Demo and unboxing videos**: Show the product being used in real situations. Genuine use builds more trust than perfectly staged videos
- **Review-style videos**: First-person, casual, honest delivery consistently outperforms scripted marketing language on TikTok
- **Before/after formats**: Especially effective for beauty, cleaning, fitness, and home products

**The First Two Seconds Are Everything**

TikTok's algorithm measures completion rate and engagement within the first moments of a video. If you don't hook the viewer in the first two seconds, they scroll — and TikTok stops distributing your video. Open with a surprising visual, a bold statement, a provocative question, or an action already in progress. Never start with your brand logo or a slow pan.

**A Note on Audio Quality**

On TikTok, bad audio is one of the biggest hidden reasons viewers lose interest. If people can't hear you clearly in a demo, voiceover, or live session, trust drops, and watch time falls. Both of these affect how widely TikTok shows your content. For sellers making videos or going live without a studio, a small wireless mic can help a lot.

On TikTok, realness beats polished production, but clear, easy-to-understand audio is the minimum standard your content must meet.

### How to Go Live on TikTok Shop (TikTok LIVE Selling)?

TikTok LIVE shopping is one of the highest-converting formats on the platform. A well-run LIVE session creates urgency, allows for real-time Q&A, and can generate significant revenue in a single broadcast.

**Setup Steps**

1. Confirm you meet the **1,000-follower minimum** required for TikTok LIVE access in the US (requirements vary by market — check your regional Seller Center for current thresholds).
2. In Seller Center, navigate to **LIVE** and enable **Product Showcase** to link your product catalog to your LIVE sessions.
3. In the TikTok app, tap the **+** button → **LIVE** → select products to pin from your catalog before going live.
